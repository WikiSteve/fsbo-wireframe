Pilot overview

The electronic agent orchestrates a seven-day FSBO sprint by combining rules-based validation with lawyer-controlled identity and trust processes. This concept wireframe highlights the product experience that keeps the seller within the private seller exemption while delivering the documentation needed for a future B2B rollout.

How acceptance works

The Owner has pre-set objective rules for acceptable offers (price, closing window, and conditions). If your offer passes those rules, you’ll be sent to the Owner’s law firm to (1) verify your identity and (2) pay the deposit into the firm’s trust account. When the law firm confirms both (a) identity verification and (b) cleared deposit, the Owner’s electronic agent will communicate the Owner’s acceptance on screen and by email with the fully executed Agreement of Purchase and Sale. No negotiation is available through this site.

Represented buyers and agents

This is a private sale. No cooperating commission is offered. Represented buyers may submit through this portal. Any compensation owed to a brokerage is between that buyer and their brokerage under their buyer agreement.
